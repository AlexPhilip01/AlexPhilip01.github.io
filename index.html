Cover Letter

Dear Recruiter,

I am a cybersecurity professional with a Master’s degree in Information Security from Royal Holloway University of London and hands-on experience in cybercrime investigation, security monitoring, and incident response. I am currently seeking opportunities where I can apply my technical knowledge and analytical skills to help organizations strengthen their security posture and respond effectively to evolving cyber threats.

During my internship with the Department of Cybercrime, Government of India, I worked on investigations involving phishing, fraud, and identity theft. I utilized digital forensics tools such as Autopsy and FTK to support cybercrime investigations and collaborated with law enforcement teams on incident response and threat mitigation activities. I also contributed to cybersecurity awareness initiatives and training programs for officers, helping strengthen operational security practices.

In addition to my formal experience, I have completed hands-on investigations through platforms such as Blue Team Labs Online and TryHackMe, where I analyzed real-world security scenarios involving log analysis, threat detection, and incident investigation. These experiences have strengthened my practical understanding of both offensive and defensive security techniques.

My academic and practical experience has provided me with a strong foundation in network security, cryptography, threat analysis, and secure system monitoring. I am certified in CompTIA Security+ and CompTIA CySA+, reinforcing my knowledge of security operations, risk management, and threat detection. I also have experience using Python and Bash for automation and troubleshooting.

Beyond technical expertise, I value structured problem-solving, clear communication, and continuous learning. I am particularly interested in roles involving security monitoring, incident response, threat detection, and security engineering within dynamic environments.

I would welcome the opportunity to contribute my skills and enthusiasm to an organization committed to building resilient and secure systems.

Thank you for your time and consideration.

Kind regards,
Alex Amitraj Philip
