How to embed your LinkedIn posts
01
Go to your LinkedIn post
Open the post you want to embed on LinkedIn. Click the three dots (···) in the top-right corner of the post.
02
Click "Embed this post"
From the dropdown menu, select "Embed this post". LinkedIn will generate an iframe embed code for that specific post.
03
Copy the iframe code
Copy the entire code. It will look like this:
<iframe src="https://www.linkedin.com/embed/feed/update/urn:li:share:XXXXXXXXXX" height="570" width="504" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="" title="Embedded post"></iframe>
04
Open linkedin-posts.html and find the post slot
In this file, find the post card you want to update. Inside <div class="post-embed">, you will see a <div class="post-placeholder"> block.
05
Replace the placeholder with the iframe
Delete the entire <div class="post-placeholder">...</div> block and paste your copied iframe code in its place. Change width="504" to width="100%" so it fills the card.
<!-- BEFORE --> <div class="post-placeholder"> ... </div> <!-- AFTER --> <iframe src="https://www.linkedin.com/embed/feed/update/urn:li:share:XXXXXXXXXX" height="570" width="100%" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>
06
Update the post metadata
Also update the date, topics, description, and "View on LinkedIn" link URL in the same post card to match your actual post. Then save and upload the file.
