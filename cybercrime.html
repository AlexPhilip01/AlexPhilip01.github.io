During my internship with the Cybercrime Department of the Government of India, I gained practical exposure to real-world cybercrime investigations and digital forensic analysis. I assisted in analyzing cyber incidents such as phishing attacks, financial fraud, and identity theft cases. As part of the investigation process, I worked with digital evidence and learned how to examine logs, trace suspicious activities, and support investigative workflows using forensic tools. This experience strengthened my understanding of incident response procedures, cyber threat investigation, and the importance of maintaining evidence integrity during forensic analysis. The internship also enhanced my analytical thinking and problem-solving skills while working alongside law enforcement professionals in addressing cybersecurity incidents.
